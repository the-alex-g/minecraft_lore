Obtaining

Spindles can be crafted from a stick and cobblestone, blackstone, or cobbled deepslate. They can also be found in Shepherd village chests.

Usage

Using a spindle in your main hand while holding wool in your offhand converts the wool into string one at a time, which is how textiles actually work in real life. You can then craft the string back into wool and eventually use all your wool and string and get nothing in return but a damaged spindle.
