Obtaining

Lithozoas can be mined with any tool, but pickaxes are the fastest. They generate naturally in most overworld surface biomes other than dense forests.

Lithozoas can be crafted using lithozoa tendrils and cobblestone or equivalent materials.

Usage

When placed, lithozoas change color depending on their biome and begin sliding up and down until they reach a height of 2 to 4 blocks above the ground.

If a player walks under a lithozoa, it unleashes tentacles. A player caught in the tentacles has their horizontal movement speed halved and takes 2 damage per tick, which I'm pretty sure is 2 damage every 0.5 seconds counting damage immunity.

Lithozoa tendrils can be mined with any tool, but always drop as an item when shears or silk touch are used. Swords and shears mine the tendrils the fastest. Once a lithozoa's tendrils have been broken three times, it retracts its tendrils and waits between one and two minutes before extending its tendrils again.

Lithozoa tendrils can be climbed at 3/4 of the player's normal vertical movement rate.
