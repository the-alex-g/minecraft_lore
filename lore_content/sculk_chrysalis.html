Obtaining

Sculk chrysalises can be obtained by mining them with a silk touch tool, sculk hoe, or deep hoe. Any other method of mining drops 1-3 sculk silk, which is primarily used to craft enchanted books with Silk Touch.

Usage

When placed on the bottom of a block, a sculk chrysalis block is placed as usual. When used on any other block face, a tamed Sculk Prowler is spawned as if placed by a spawn egg.
