Changes

Wandering traders have a 10% chance to spawn as sculk traders. Sculk traders have a different texture and have sculk-related trades instead of the usual ones.

Wandering traders also sell an additional item, which is from Alex's mod. Possibilities include hostile mob essences, bluegourd seeds or slices, berries found in the overworld, dark sea urchins, aura seeds or disks, lapis roses, lithozoa, rune spires, or cave fish in a bucket.
