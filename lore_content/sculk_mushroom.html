Obtaining

Sculk Mushrooms can be instantly mined by hand or with any tool, and are broken by water and pistons.

When a sculk vein converts another block into sculk, if the vein is above a sculk block, there is a 0.486% chance that the sculk vein will be converted into a sculk mushroom.

Sculk Mushrooms spread over time following the same rules as regular red and brown mushrooms.

Usage

Sculk Mushrooms can be placed on any sculk block and any block vanilla mushrooms can grow on.

Using bonemeal on a sculk mushroom has a 40% chance to grow a huge sculk mushroom composed of sculk stalks and echoing caps. Echoing caps can be crafted into most wood-based items.

A sculk mushroom, shrillstalk, sculk vein, and bowl can be crafted into sculk stew.
