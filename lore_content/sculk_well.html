Obtaining

A sculk well drops itself when mined with a silk touch tool or either a Sculk Hoe or Deep Hoe. Otherwise, it drops 5 experience when broken.

When a block is converted to sculk, if the block above is air, there is a 0.3% chance that the block is converted to a full sculk well. This chance is increased to 0.9% during world generation.

Behavior

Naturally generated sculk wells start as full. Using an empty bottle on a full sculk well empties the well and replaces a bottle with a Bottle of Sculk Lymph.

When sculk spreads into an empty sculk well, there is a small chance that the well refills itself.

A mob that walks into a full sculk well is given the Wasting and Darkness effects for 10 seconds.
