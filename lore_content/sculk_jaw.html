Obtaining

Sculk jaws can be obtained by mining them with a silk touch tool or either a Sculk Hoe or a Deep Hoe. Otherwise, it drops 5 experience.

Triggered and untriggered sculk jaws drop seperate items that retain the triggered state. Triggered sculk jaws are sometimes sold by sculk traders.

Behavior

When an entity steps on top of an untriggered sculk jaw, it triggers, dealing 8 points of damage and a good bit of knockback. A triggered sculk jaw has a 10% chance of resetting itself when given a random tick.

Using a Sculk Key on a sculk jaw toggles its triggered state. Shift-using a key on a jaw locks or unlocks it. A locked sculk jaw displays a glyph on the block's sides and does not trigger when stepped on or reset naturally.
