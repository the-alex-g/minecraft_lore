Obtaining

Vigilant sculk can be mined with a silk touch tool or a deep or sculk hoe. Any other tool drops no item and 5 experience.

Vigilant sculk generates rarely in the Deep Dark and can be crafted using eight soul sculk, a wither skeleton skull, and a sculk shrieker.

Usage

When a sculk sensor or calibrated sculk sensor on top of a vigilant sculk activates, the vigilant sculk attacks the entity that caused the vibration. This attack uses the sonic boom particles but is actually a magic attack. It deals 5 damage, hitting targets even through blocks. Each vibration-dampening block the attack travels through reduces the total damage by 2/3. After firing, there is a delay of between 1.5 and 3 seconds before the vigilant sculk can fire again.

Vigilant sculk also fires at the closest non-sculk entity when receiving a random tick or when a sculk sensor directly above it is destroyed.
