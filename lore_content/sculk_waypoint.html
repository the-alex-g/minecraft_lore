Obtaining

Sculk waypoints can be mined with any tool, but hoes mine them fastest. They can be crafted from sculk nuggets, a sculk ingot, and a sculk light.

Usage

Interacting with a placed sculk waypoint toggles its lit state. When active, it glows with a light level of 10 and also displays a white cube that can be seen through other blocks similar to mobs affected with the Glowing effect.
