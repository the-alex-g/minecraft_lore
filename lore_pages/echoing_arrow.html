echoing arrow

Obtaining

Echoing Arrows Can Be Crafted Using A Stick, Feather, And Echo Shard. They Can Also Be Found Rarely In Ancient City Loot Chests.

Usage

Half A Second After Firing An Echoing Arrow From A Bow, A Second Arrow Is Fired In The Same Direction As The Old One. The Second Arrow Deals 2/3 Of The Damage Of The Original Arrow.
