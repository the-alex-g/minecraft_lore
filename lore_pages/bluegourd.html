Bluegourd

Obtaining

Bluegourds drop themselves when mined with a silk touch tool. Any other tool causes them to drop between 3 and 7 bluegourd slices. Fortune increases the number of bluegourd drops, to a maximum of nine. Axes are the best tool for mining bluegourds.

Bluegourds naturally generate on the ground of lush caves.

Usage

Bluegourds can be smelted into iron nuggets and are broken when pushed by pistons.

Bluegourd seeds can only be planted on moss and clay.
