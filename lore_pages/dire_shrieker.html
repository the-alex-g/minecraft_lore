Dire Shrieker

Obtaining

A dire shrieker can be crafted from three obsidian and a normal sculk shrieker, and it can be mined with a silk touch tool, sculk hoe, or deep hoe.

Usage

5 seconds after a dire shrieker is placed, it emits a shriek. After another five seconds, if the region above the dire shrieker is air, an Ancient One is summoned above the shrieker and the shrieker becomes a normal, inactive sculk shrieker. If the spawn region is obstructed, the spawning is retried every 5 seconds while the dire shrieker remains intact.
