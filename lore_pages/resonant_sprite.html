Resonant Sprite

Behavior

When spawned, resonant sprites are given a single target. They continue to attack that target until it has died. While the sprite has no living target, or after it has dealt damage to its target, it has a 1% chance per tick to despawn.

Loot

When killed, a resonant sprite has a 5% chance to drop a resonant rod. The Looting enchantment increases the chance by a little bit.
