sculk lymph bottle

Obtaining

Sculk Lymph Is Obtained By Using Empty Glass Bottles On Full Sculk Wells. Sculk Lymph Also Has A Chance To Generate In Ancient City Loot Chests.

Usage

Sculk Lymph Is Primarily Used To Craft Sculk Nuggets. Using A Lymph Bottle On A Slime Converts It To A Sculk Slime, Which Behaves The Same As A Regular Slime But Inflicts The Darkness Effect For 10 Seconds When It Hits The Player.
