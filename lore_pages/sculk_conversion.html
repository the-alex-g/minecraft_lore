Sculk Conversion

Changes

Certain blocks now become new and unique sculk blocks when they would usually turn into regular sculk.

Ores

All vanilla ores now convert to sculk ores. Iron, lapis, and gold ores drop the respective nugget form of the material similar to nether gold ore.

Nylium and Mycelium

Crimson and warped nyliums, as well as mycelium, become vibrant sculk.

Soul Sand and Soil

Soul sand and soul soil convert into soul sculk.

Logs

Logs of all varieties convert into sculk logs.

Leaves

All leaves convert into sculk husks, which can be crafted from and into four sculk veins.

Melons

Melon-type blocks convert into sculk melons. Carved pumpkins and bluegourds, as well as the glowing variants, turn into carved sculk melons.

Shroomlights

Shroomlights convert into sculk lights.
