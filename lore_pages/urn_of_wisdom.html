Urn Of Wisdom

Obtaining

An urn of wisdom can be mined with any tool or by hand, but using a pickaxe is the fastest.

Urns of wisdom can only be found in ancient city loot chests.

Usage

An urn of wisdom can store up to 100 experience points. When broken, it releases all of its stored experience. Interacting with a placed urn of wisdom puts 5 of your experience into the urn. The runes on the side of the urn glow brighter based on the amount of experience stored.
