Deep Tools

Obtaining

The full set of deep tools and armor can only be obtained by applying deep upgrade templates to sculk tools.

Usage

Deep tools are a netherite-tier item with lower durability and less damage but much higher mining speed than netherite tools.

Deep armor is slightly less tough and durable than netherite armor.

Deep leggings, hoes, pickaxes, and swords have the same special properties as their sculk counterparts, although deep pickaxes mine reinforced deepslate faster than sculk pickaxes.
