Soul Sculk

Obtaining

Soul Sculk drops itself when mined with a silk touch tool or either a Sculk Hoe or a Deep Hoe. Otherwise, it drops one experience when broken.

When a block is converted to sculk, there is a 0.1% chance that it becomes a block of soul sculk. If the block was Soul Sand or Soul Soil, there is a 100% chance instead.

Usage

Soul sculk can be used to craft soul torches and soul campfires. Walking on soul sculk also grants the speed bonus from Soul Speed, and it burns with soul fire instead of regular fire.
