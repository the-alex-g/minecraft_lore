umbral schist

Obtaining

Umbral Schist Can Be Mined With Any Pickaxe. Mining It Without A Pickaxe Drops Nothing. It Naturally Generates Between Y=0 And The Bottom Of The World.

Usage

Umbral Schist Can Be Crafted Into A Polished Variant As Well As Slabs, Walls, And Stairs

When Lava Flows Between Blue Ice And An Umbral Schist Block, It Solidifies Into A Block Of Deepslate.
