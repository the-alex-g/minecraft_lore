Warplate

Obtaining

All pieces of a set of warplate armor are crafted from adamant quartz. Adamant quartz is crafted from sorrowful quartz and a fermented troll berry. Sorrowful quartz is crafted from regular quartz and a ghast tear. Bastion treasure chests have a chance to contain sorrowful or adamant quartz.

Usage

While wearing a full set of warplate with the chestplate less than 25% damaged, you gain Haste, Speed, and Jump Boost. If the chestplate is more than 66% damaged, you gain Slowness II and Mining Fatigue II instead. Regardless of damage level, you also get Resistance. Depending on the damage level, you also receive various levels of Strength.

Warplate chestplates cannot be enchanted with Mending.
