redstone collector

Obtaining

Redstone Collectors Can Be Crafted From Four Stone And One Redstone Dust. Once Placed, The Block Cannot Be Recovered.

Usage

Redstone Collectors Have Five Stages Of Age. When Placed, They Are At Stage One. When The Collector Receives A Random Tick, It Has A 1/3 Chance To Increase Its Age By One. When Broken, A Redstone Collector Drops A Number Of Redstone Dust Equal To Its Current Age.
