Sculk Chestplate Upgrades

Obtaining

Sculk chestplate upgrade templates can be found in ancient city loot chests and also duplicated using seven diamonds and a sculk block.

Usage

Sculk chestplate upgrade templates can be combined with sculk chestplates and either a sculk catalyst, shrieker, or sensor to obtain the corresponding type of chestplate.

Catalyzing Chestplate

Crafted from a sculk catalyst, catalyzing chestplates repair all other sculk-related items worn or wielded by the player when the chestplate is damaged. Catalyzing chestplates cannot be given the mending enchantment.

Shrieking Chestplate

Shrieking chestplates are crafted from a sculk shrieker. When the player is damaged by an entity, that entity takes 2 thorns damage and shriek particles are emitted at their location. Unlike the thorns enchantment, this damage also applies to ranged attacks.

Listening Chestplate

Crafted from a sculk sensor, listening chestplates grant the player Speed II for 5 seconds when the chestplate is damaged.
