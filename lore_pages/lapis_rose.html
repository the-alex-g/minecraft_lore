Lapis Rose

Obtaining

Lapis roses can be crafted from three lapis lazuli and any small flower. They can also be found rarely in plains, meadows, and flower forests. Lapis roses can be instantly mined with any tool and are broken by water and pistons.

Usage

Lapis roses can be crafted into blue dye. When given a random tick, lapis roses have a 1/3 chance to turn the block below them into azure dirt. If that block is already azure dirt and the block two below the rose is a dirt-type block, that block will be turned into azure dirt instead.
