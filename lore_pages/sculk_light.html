Sculk Light

Obtaining

Sculk lights can be obtained using any silk touch tool or either a deep hoe or sculk hoe. Hoes are the fastest tool for breaking sculk lights.

When shroomlights are converted into sculk, they become sculk lights.

Usage

Sculk lights glow at light level 10, but reduce their glow strength in response to nearby vibrations. Vibrations of different frequencies reduce the glow strength by different amounts.

A comparator can read the current glow strength of a sculk light, outputting a redstone signal of 1.5 times the strength of the sculk light.
