Vibrant Sculk

Obtaining

Vibrant sculk drops itself when mined using silk touch, a sculk hoe, or a deep hoe. Otherwise, it drops one experience.

Vibrant sculk generates when nylium or mycelium is converted into sculk.

Usage

Using bonemeal on a block of vibrant sculk generates a patch of sculk mold, sculk fungus, shrillstalks, and echoing growths.

Using bonemeal on blocks of sculk within one block of a vibrant sculk causes the sculk block to become vibrant sculk.

Vibrant sculk blocks become regular sculk blocks when they receive a random tick with a light-occluding block above them.
