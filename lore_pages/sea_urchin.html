Sea Urchin

Obtaining

Sea urchins come in three colors (red, purple, and dark), which naturally generate in all ocean biomes. Urchins can be instantly broken with any tool or by hand.

Urchins spread following similar rules to mushrooms. However, they will only spread to blocks with water, and only if there are fewer than six urchins within a 9x3x9 rectangle centered on the first urchin and there is at least one urchin within that rectangle that is the same color as the original urchin.

Usage

Stepping on an urchin or breaking one with an empty hand deals one damage to the player. The stepping damage can be avoided by sneaking.

When receiving a random tick while out of water, urchins die and become urchin tests. Tests (which is the term for a sea urchin's internal skeleton thing) have a chance to break when stepped on similar to turtle eggs. Nine urchin tests can be crafted into sand.
