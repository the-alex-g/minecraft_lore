Sculk Vibrum

Obtaining

Sculk vibrums can be obtained with any silk touch tool, sculk hoe, or deep hoe. Mining a vibrum without any of those tools drops 1-3 resonant rods, modified by Fortune.

Sculk vibrums have a 1/3 chance of generating instead of a sculk shrieker when the growth is on top of resonant sculk.

Usage

All sculk vibrums created during world generation and 33% of those created afterwards summon resonant sprites when receiving a vibration from a sculk sensor. There is a 10% chance for the vibrum to summon two sprites; otherwise, it summons one. The resonant sprite(s) target the entity that caused the vibration.
