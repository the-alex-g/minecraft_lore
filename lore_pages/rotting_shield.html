Rotting Shield

Obtaining

Rotting shields can be crafted from eight rotting steel and one rotting trap.

Usage

Rotting shields can be used as normal shields with a durability of 240, which is 76 less than a regular shield. When blocking an attack with a rotting shield, the attacker is given Poison for 5 seconds. If the poison effect is successfully applied, you heal 0.25.

Rotting shields can be repaired with rotting steel or rotten flesh.
