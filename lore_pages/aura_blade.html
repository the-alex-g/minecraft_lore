Aura Blade

Obtaining

Aura blades can be crafted from eight aura disks.

Usage

Using an aura blade throws it as a projectile that deals 5 damage on a hit. Once the aura blade strikes a target or the ground, it has a 90% chance to drop as an item again.

Aura blades can be enchanted. Each level of Unbreaking reduces the chance of breaking by 90%. Each level of Sharpness increases the damage by 0.5, except the first level, which increases the damage by 1. Enchanting the aura blade with Loyalty causes it to return to your inventory after one second instead of dropping an item. Each level of loyalty past the first reduces the return time by 0.5 seconds, to a minimum of one tick.

Aura blades can be smelted or blasted into gold nuggets.

Dispensers fire aura blades like arrows.
