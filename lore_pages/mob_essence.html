Mob Essence

Obtaining

Mob essences can be obtained by killing most hostile mobs. Using a essence extractor yields an additional essence and can also be used to get essences of passive mobs. Wandering traders have a 15% chance to trade a random hostile mob essence.

Usage

Eight matching essences and an egg can be used to craft a spawn egg for whatever mob yielded the essences.
