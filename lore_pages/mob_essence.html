mob essence

Obtaining

Mob Essences Can Be Obtained By Killing Most Hostile Mobs. Using A Essence Extractor Yields An Additional Essence And Can Also Be Used To Get Essences Of Passive Mobs.

The Following Mobs Have Essences. Essences Marked With A * Can Only Be Obtained Using An Essence Extractor.

Usage

Eight Matching Essences And An Egg Can Be Used To Craft A Spawn Egg For Whatever Mob Yielded The Essences.
