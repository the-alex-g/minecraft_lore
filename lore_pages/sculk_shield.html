Sculk Shield

Obtaining

Sculk shields can be crafted from six reinforced sculk, a sculk ingot, and a sculk sensor.

Usage

Sculk shields can be used as normal shields with a durability of 312, 24 less than a normal shield. When blocking an attack, you gain Strength for 1.5 seconds.

Sculk ingots, sculk sensors, reinforced sculk, sculk, and sculk logs can all be used to repair sculk shields.
