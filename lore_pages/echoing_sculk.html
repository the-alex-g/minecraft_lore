Echoing Sculk

Obtaining

Echoing Sculk can be obtained by mining it with a tool with Silk Touch or either a Deep Hoe or a Sculk Hoe. Echoing sculk is mined fastest by hoes.

Echoing Sculk has a 0.5% chance to generate instead of a normal sculk block when sculk spreads. When an Echoing Charge hits a Sculk block, there is a 1% chance that the sculk block converts to echoing sculk.

Behavior

When mined with a tool other than a Deep or Sculk Hoe, echoing sculk has a 90% chance to relocate instead of being mined. Each level of Fortune on the tool reduces the relocation chance by 5%.

Echoing sculk can relocate to the top of any sculk, reinforced sculk, or vibrant sculk block as long as the new location is empty. The echoing sculk can teleport to any space within two blocks horizontally and one block vertically. If no valid teleport location is found, the echoing sculk breaks.

When mined without a silk touch tool or sculk hoe, echoing sculk drops an Echo Shard instead of itself.
