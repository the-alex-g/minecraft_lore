Berry Picker

Obtaining

Berry pickers can be crafted out of four planks, any berry, two iron ingots, and a redstone. They can be mined with any tool, but axes are the fastest. One berry picker naturally generates in berry houses.

Usage

If the berry picker is facing a berry plant block, whenever the berries on the plant become fully ripe, the berry picker will pick the berries as though the player had used the berry bush.
