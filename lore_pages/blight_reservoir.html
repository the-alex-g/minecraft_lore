Blight Reservoir

Obtaining

Blight reservoirs can be obtained only by mining them with a Silk Touch tool. Using any other tool causes them to explode. Up to four blight reservoirs generate in the basements of rotting spires, with each one having a 50% chance of generating.

Usage

Blight reservoirs contain 4 charges. Using a glass bottle on a blight reservoir consumes one charge and turns a glass bottle into a blight grenade. Using a blight grenade on a reservoir turns the grenade back into a glass bottle and adds a charge to the reservoir.

When an entity falls on a blight reservoir from a height greater than 1 block, the reservoir explodes.

Explosion

Blight reservoirs explode with an explosion strength equal to the number of charges left in the reservoir plus one.
