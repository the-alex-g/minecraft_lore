Aura Seed

Obtaining

Aura seeds can be obtained by breaking aura stem or through crafting. An apple and four gold ingots can be crafted into an aura seed, as can aura disks.

Usage

Aura seeds can be planted on any block of quartz. Once an aura stem has matured, there is a 20% chance to generate an aura disk above it if the block above is air when it receives a random tick.
