Shrillstalk

Obtaining

Shrillstalks can be instantly mined by hand or with any tool, and are broken by water and pistons.

When a sculk vein converts another block into sculk, if the vein is above a sculk block, there is a 0.054% chance that the sculk vein will be converted into a shrillstalk.

Shrillstalks spread following the same rules as regular red and brown mushrooms.

Usage

Shrillstalks can be placed on any sculk block and any block vanilla mushrooms can grow on.

Using bonemeal on a shrillstalk has a 40% chance to grow a huge shrillstalk composed of shrilling stalks and echoing shelves.
