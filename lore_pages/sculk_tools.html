Sculk Tools

Obtaining

The full set of deep tools and armor can be crafted from sculk ingots and echo shards.

Usage

Sculk tools are a diamond-tier item with far less durability but much higher mining speed than diamond tools.

Sculk armor is much less durable than diamond armor, but provides a small amount of knockback resistance.

Sculk Hoes

A sculk hoe mines sculk-related blocks as though it were enchanted with Silk Touch.

Sculk Pickaxe

Reinforced deepslate drops itself when mined with a sculk pickaxe.

Sculk Leggings

While wearing sculk leggings, you gain a bonus to sneaking speed.

Sculk Sword

When you kill a mob with a sculk sword, the sword gains a charge. When the sword is fully charged, you can use it like a bow to fire a sonic boom.
